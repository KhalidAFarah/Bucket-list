Our main focus

GreenRoutine is an app that gives you points for recycling garbage through a specially designed garbage box that sorts the different types of waste. You get points based on the weight of trash you recycle, and then the set of points that you get is calculated based on the weight of each bag of thrash that is put into our special designed garbage box. The points system is a competition between the users in your community that uses the app, and you can use the points you earn at various stores to either get gifts or discounts.
