GreenRoutine is both an app and a specially designed recycling machine made to both encourage and give people a reason to recycle the garbage and clean up in their communities. By recycling trash, you can earn points.


The points you get is calculated based on the weight of each bag of trash that is put into our special designed garbage box and then sent into the app. The points you earn can be used at various stores to get discounts, and or you can compete between friends in your community to recycle and gather the most points.
