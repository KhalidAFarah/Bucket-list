Solution

This is an app based on how a singular person can contribute to lessen the strain on the environment.


We are making this app to reduce the environmental crisis from this point in time. The app has following rules:


1. When you're finished downloading this app, you will get a personal scan code on your phone that you can use to scan different kind of garbage. One person can scan a maximum of 3 garbage goods each day.


2. This is a competition between all green list-users based on who gets the most points week after week.
